Location

BAC is located on Broadwater Road, Mansfield and Wishart Road, Wishart. Parking for Carols by Candlelight is provided on the College grounds. The entrance for this gate 4 on Wishart Road. The grounds are beautiful with lush green playing fields and bushland with a creek running through the middle. You can see on the map below that the carols will be held on playing field 1 near the main building and basketball court.
