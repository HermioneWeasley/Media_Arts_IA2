Welcome

Welcome to the BAC Carols website. Here you will find all of the details about this wonderful event. The carols will run from 5:30pm to 9:00pm. Food trucks will be open to take orders and serve food and drinks from 5:30pm to 7:30p. There will be market stalls which will run from 6:00pm to 8:00pm and rides that will run from 6:00pm to 8:30pm. If you want to listen to the speech and carols they will be at 7:30pm to 9:00pm.
