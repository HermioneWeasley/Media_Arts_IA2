Information

The BAC Carols by Candlilight are a highlight of the College calendar, eagerly anticipated by students and their families, teachers, and the wider community. At the BAC carols by candlelight event their will be food trucks where you can buy food and drinks. There will also be market stalls to purchase various items from student art works and gifts to crafts. Being a Christian College, the highlight of the event is the carols which focus on the birth of our saviour Jesus Christ. A speaker will be at the event who will talk about Christmas and the meaning of it. After the speaker there will be carols for everyone to join in. There will also be some fun fair rides were you will be able to purchase a ticket to ride on the rides.

The poster on the right contains the key details of the Carols including the date and starting time. Feel free to post this on social media so your friends and family can remember to attend.
